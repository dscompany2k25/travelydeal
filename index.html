FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TravelyDeal.

How does TravelyDeal work?
TravelyDeal monitors fares across 300+ airlines 24/7. When you set a price alert for any route, we notify you by email the moment fares drop below your target price. You can also browse trending deals and search flights directly on our platform.
Is TravelyDeal free to use?
Yes! Our Free plan gives you access to flight search and trending deals. For unlimited price alerts, AI recommendations, and premium features upgrade to Pro (€9/month) or Premium (€19/month). No credit card required to start.
How many price alerts can I set?
The Free plan includes 2 active price alerts. Pro subscribers get unlimited alerts for any route worldwide. Premium users get unlimited alerts plus family sharing for up to 5 people on the same account.
Which airlines does TravelyDeal cover?
We monitor 300+ airlines worldwide, including TAP Air Portugal, Ryanair, EasyJet, Iberia, British Airways, Lufthansa, Air France, Emirates, and all major low-cost and full-service carriers operating to and from Portugal.
How quickly will I receive a price alert?
Alerts are sent in real-time — typically within minutes of a fare change. We scan prices every 15 minutes across all monitored routes. Pro and Premium subscribers receive priority delivery to ensure you never miss a flash deal.
Does TravelyDeal book flights directly?
No. TravelyDeal is a fare tracking and alert service. When you find a deal, we redirect you directly to the airline's website or a trusted booking platform to complete your purchase. This means you always book at the best available price with no added fees.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard with no penalties. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. EU residents are also entitled to a 14-day cooling-off period. See our Refund Policy for details.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor — the highest level of certification available. We never store your card number or sensitive payment data on our servers.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us at admin@travelydeal.com or call +351 932 004 691. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Friday, 9h–18h WET).
