Cookie Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026  ·  Applies to: travelydeal.com

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter information every time you visit.

We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage. This policy covers all such technologies under the term "cookies."

2. How We Use Cookies

TravelyDeal uses cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and search settings
  • Understand how visitors use our platform (analytics)
  • Track affiliate referrals via our Travelpayouts partnership
  • Comply with our legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Categories of Cookies We Use

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality such as login sessions and security features. No consent is required for essential cookies under the EU ePrivacy Directive.

4. Travelpayouts Tracking Script

Our site loads a tracking script from Travelpayouts (emrldtp.com) to enable affiliate link attribution. This script may set cookies or use similar tracking technologies on your device when you interact with flight deal links. The script operates under Travelpayouts' Privacy Policy, available at their website.

We only load this script when you have accepted "All" cookies via our cookie consent banner. If you choose "Essential only," this script is not activated.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 TravelyDeal Consent Banner

When you first visit TravelyDeal, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose:

  • Accept all: Enables all cookie categories including analytics and marketing cookies.
  • Essential only: Only essential cookies that are required for the site to function will be set.

Your preference is stored in localStorage under the key td_consent. You can withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or cookies and reloading the page.

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may prevent some features of TravelyDeal from working correctly. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

5.3 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics opt-out: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Your Online Choices (EU): youronlinechoices.eu

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. TravelyDeal does not currently respond to DNT signals as there is no industry-standard protocol. You can use the cookie controls described above to manage tracking preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in the technologies we use or in applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where required, by re-displaying the consent banner.
